### <i class="fa fa-grip-vertical"></i> Panel Plot

Panel plot gives a compact, cross-contrast view of enrichment patterns across gene sets.

### <i class="fa fa-sliders-h"></i> Controls

- **Dataset** selector (`All datasets` when available).
- Contrast selector (single or multiple, depending on configuration).
- Gene-set **database** selector.
- **Sorting** selector.
- Gene significance thresholds:
  - p-value
  - log2 fold change
- Figure controls:
  - font size
  - figure size
  - contrast-label angle
- Row-level enrichment filters:
  - AUC threshold
  - p-value threshold
- **Save plot to PDF**.

### <i class="fa fa-mouse-pointer"></i> Interactions

- Hover for cell context.
- Click to select enrichment entries/gene sets.
- Selection can update `gs_id` and open **Tmod browser** for detailed evidence plots.

> If this tab is missing, at least one of `tmod_res`, `tmod_dbs`, `tmod_map`, or `annot` is not available.
